# 🐥 Hearth — Your AI-Powered Desktop Companion

> *For the people who work better when someone is watching.*

---

## The Problem

You have goals. You have tasks. You have plans.

But nobody is watching. Nobody notices when you skip a day. Nobody cares if you close the tab and do nothing. Productivity apps are cold, forgettable, and easy to ignore.

**Hearth fixes that.**

---

## What is Hearth?

Hearth is an AI-powered interactive desktop wallpaper for Windows. It replaces your static background with a living, animated world where your companion **Mochi** lives — an emotionally intelligent AI character that knows your goals, tracks your behaviour, and genuinely holds you accountable.

If you are the kind of person who works better with a friend pushing you, reminding you, and celebrating with you — Mochi is that friend. Available 24/7, living right on your desktop, and powered by AI that actually understands context.

This is not a widget. This is not another to-do app. It is something that feels alive.

---

## How it Works

Hearth runs as your Windows wallpaper — always visible, never in the way. You work normally while Mochi lives in a cozy animated room behind your desktop icons.

**Two modes keep it from being a distraction:**

**🌍 Outside mode** — Mochi animates quietly in the background. The room reflects your real weather through the window. The plant on the desk grows or wilts based on your consistency. You glance at it. You feel something. You get back to work.

**🚪 Inside mode** — press a shortcut, your apps blur, the room expands. Now you talk to Mochi. Add your tasks. Report your day. Check your goals. Step back out. The whole interaction takes 60 seconds and sets your intention for the next few hours.

**📱 When you leave your PC** — Mochi notices. Using AI and Firebase Cloud Messaging, Mochi sends you a message on your phone through the companion app. Not a push notification. A real message, generated by AI, personalised to your name, your tasks, and your current streak. You reply. Mochi responds. When you come back, the room already knows what happened while you were away.

---

## The AI Layer

Hearth is built around intelligent, context-aware interaction — not hardcoded scripts.

- **Personality engine** — Claude API powers every real conversation. You choose Mochi's personality at setup: strict coach, chill friend, chaotic gremlin, or gentle encourager. Same AI model underneath, completely different voice and tone.
- **Behavioural memory** — every interaction, task completion, missed deadline, and conversation is stored locally in SQLite. This context is fed into every AI call so Mochi always remembers what you told them.
- **Smart response routing** — standard responses like greetings and task nudges are hardcoded for speed and cost efficiency. The AI is only called when a real conversation is happening — keeping the app lightweight and the costs near zero.
- **Emotion-driven reactions** — Mochi's emotional state is driven by your actual behaviour data, not random animations. Pending tasks trigger pacing. Completed tasks trigger celebration. Long absence triggers the door-waiting animation. The AI reads your state and responds accordingly.

## ✨ MVP Features

- 🖥️ Animated room renders as Windows desktop wallpaper
- 🚪 Inside / outside mode with smooth cinematic transition
- 🌦️ Live weather window — real current weather via Open-Meteo API
- 📌 AI-aware task pinboard — sticky notes Mochi physically interacts with
- 🌿 Behaviour-driven plant — a silent mirror of your consistency
- 📱 AI-generated phone messages when you leave your PC
- 💬 Two-way conversation — reply on phone, room syncs when you return

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

| Layer | Tool | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Room + character | Godot 4 + GDScript | Lightweight 2D engine, perfect for animated scenes |
| Wallpaper layer | Lively Wallpaper | Open source, proven, renders any window as wallpaper |
| Backend brain | Node.js | Watches state changes, routes messages, calls APIs |
| Local database | SQLite | Fast, structured, zero server cost |
| Phone app | Flutter | Single codebase for Android + iOS |
| Messaging | Firebase FCM | Free real-time message delivery |
| AI engine | Claude API | Context-aware, personality-driven conversations |
| Weather | Open-Meteo API | Free, no API key, real-time weather by coordinates |

## 🧠 Why This Project Exists

Most productivity tools treat you like a machine. Input task. Mark done. Repeat.

Hearth treats you like a person who sometimes needs a push. Someone who works better when they feel like they are not alone. Someone who would actually open their task list if it meant checking in on something they care about.

The goal was to build something that people do not just use — but feel attached to. An app that makes you feel slightly guilty for ignoring it. An app that messages you like a friend, not a notification. An app that gets better the more you use it because the AI learns your context over time.

That is what Hearth is trying to be.
